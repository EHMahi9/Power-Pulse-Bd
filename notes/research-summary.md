# Research Summary

The original idea from the supplied research and PDF is strong: a Bangladesh-focused loadshedding intelligence platform with a solar backup module. Fresh checking on May 8, 2026 refined the positioning.

## Refined Problem

There are official utility pages for customer-specific interruption tracking, and at least one current crowd-sourced loadshedding tracker exists. The open gap is a fuller, portfolio-grade platform that combines:

- community outage reports,
- admin verification,
- district-level dashboard metrics,
- household solar/IPS backup calculation,
- saved estimates,
- clean authentication and security,
- a path to future maps, alerts, and utility data integration.

That makes PowerPulse BD a better academic and portfolio concept than a simple CRUD app or a plain outage marker map.

## Evidence Checked

- The Daily Star reported April 2026 power outages affecting Sylhet, Noakhali, and Gazipur, including rural deficits, hospital diesel costs, and industrial disruption: https://www.thedailystar.net/news/bangladesh/news/power-outages-cripple-daily-life-three-dists-4156471
- DPDC has a customer-number based interruption tracking page, which is useful but not a national open community feed: https://dpdc.org.bd/public/home/power_interruption_tracking
- A crowd-sourced tracker called loadshed.online exists, which means PowerPulse BD should differentiate through verification, solar backup planning, course-level architecture, and long-term product polish: https://loadshed.online/

## Why This Fits Bangladesh

- Loadshedding and local interruptions still affect study, healthcare, small businesses, and factories.
- Solar home systems and IPS backups are common enough that practical backup estimation is valuable.
- A student project can start with a narrow Stage 1 feature set and later grow into a stronger civic utility.

## Recommended Project Positioning

Use this one-line pitch:

```text
PowerPulse BD helps Bangladeshi households and small businesses report local power outages, view community electricity updates, and estimate solar/IPS backup time for essential appliances.
```
